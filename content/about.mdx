I got into this because I love making things. I still do.

My background is in motion — title sequences, broadcast, VFX — but what I'm really interested in is the whole picture. The strategy behind the image. The system behind the moment. The feeling a brand creates before someone can explain why.

I believe play is methodology. That the audience's experience is the only honest measure of whether something is working. That good design should make you feel understood.

I've built brand worlds for Sanrio and Care Bears. Directed graphics for documentaries that changed how I see the world. Helped a healthcare startup feel less like a hospital and more like a new beginning.

I'm especially interested in what comes next — AI-assisted production, smaller teams doing bigger work, the tools that are changing what's possible.

Currently available for creative direction, brand identity, and visual systems work.

Let's build something.
